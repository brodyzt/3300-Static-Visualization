Female Representation in Movies

Overview

Inherent issues in society are often mirrored in said society's contemporary media. Due to this fact, taking a more detailed and in depth look into media can illustrate these prevalent patterns. In this data visualization, we seek to illustrate the differences in various factors between movies that pass and fail the Bechdel Test, a test that evaluates whether or not the movie in question contains an instance of two women talking about something other than men, viewing the data at different angles to reveal patterns and trends that illuminate how American society viewed women in the past decades.
